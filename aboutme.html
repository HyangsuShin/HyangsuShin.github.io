"About Me"


I am a junior game developer with 5 years of experience in mechanical engineering. My interest in math and physics brought me into the world of game programming, and I want to start a new career as a game developer.

Throughout my Game Programming courses, I have demonstrated my superior skills in C++ by creating games in programming fundamentals, SDL, OpenGL, and network classes. I am very much interested in fixing programming errors one by one to improve the quality of the game, and in my classes, the result of figuring out the reason of the bug was a perfect score on my assignments. In addition, I have learned how to use Phaser and Unity to make games. To edit designs, I used Gimp and Blender. In my portfolio, I list details about each class's project.

Having worked on a variety of team projects, I am well accustomed to working collaboratively. Throughout my career as a mechanical engineer, I worked with a variety of teams to build car paint factories. I demonstrated collaborative skills as I always encouraged active communication and offered detailed feedback to ensure that I kept my teams united and working towards our common goal. I shared scheduling and notifications with other teams right away to ensure development was completed on time, and the schedule sheet I created then became the company's standard template.

I have had the opportunity to travel extensively throughout my life, and these experiences have enhanced my creativity as a developer by exploring new perspectives.

I am ready to take the leap and continue to hone my skills with the right company. By contributing to the development of an innovative game world in the future, I intend to offer solutions to problems and create a more complete play.
